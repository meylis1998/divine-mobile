## Project Notes

Switched FFmpeg from software encoding (libx264) to hardware-accelerated encoding:
- iOS/macOS: h264_videotoolbox (VideoToolbox)
- Android: h264_mediacodec (MediaCodec)
- Desktop fallback: libx264 -preset ultrafast

Updated 6 FFmpeg calls in vine_recording_controller.dart. All now use _getVideoEncoderArgs() helper.

This should give 5-10x speedup on mobile. Hardware encoders don't use -preset option.
